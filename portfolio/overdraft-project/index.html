New Product Landing Page Mock Up: LendUp - August 2019

I was approached by the Lead Product Manager at LendUp, with a request to design a landing page that customers would be directed to for a new product that was being developed. The goal was to create a page that clearly communicated all the features of the product to the customer.

Before I began my initial sketch, I did some research to see how similar products in the market are designed.

I thought the graphic in the first image, and that the messaging in the second image were both very effective in communicating their message. So, I used InVision to create a similar image, to go along with my wireframe.

When it came to designing the wireframe for the page, my process was mobile-first.

Once I finished this, I translated it into a desktop version.

(These are the final mockups. In reality, there were a few wireframes sent to the product team that they tested, then came back to me with feedback. I would make the necessary modifications, and send them back)

In the end, the product team was extremely happy with these mockups, and this along with the created “text conversation” image I created, were very helpful with pushing this product forward.
